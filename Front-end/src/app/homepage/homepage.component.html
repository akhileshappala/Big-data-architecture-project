FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
What is food surplus management?Food surplus management involves the efficient handling and distribution of excess food to minimize waste and ensure it reaches those in need.
Why is food surplus management important?Food surplus management is crucial in reducing food waste, alleviating hunger, and promoting sustainability by redirecting surplus food to those who can benefit from it.
How can our product help with food surplus management?Our product provides a user-friendly platform to track, manage, and redistribute surplus food efficiently, connecting donors with organizations in need.
Is food surplus management cost-effective?Yes, effective food surplus management can lead to cost savings for businesses, reduce disposal expenses, and contribute to a positive social and environmental impact.
What types of organizations can benefit from food surplus management?Various organizations such as University Dinings, Restaurants, Grocery stores, event venues, and food banks can benefit from implementing food surplus management practices to reduce waste and support their communities.
